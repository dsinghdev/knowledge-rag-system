Kisan Vikas Patra (KVP) - Scheme Details

(1) Who can open:-
(i) a single adult
(ii) Joint Account (up to 3 adults)
(iii) a guardian on behalf of minor or on behalf of person of unsound mind
(iv) a minor above 10 years in his own name.

(2) Deposit:-
(i) Minimum ₹ 1000/- and in multiple of ₹ 100/- No maximum limit.
(ii) Any number of accounts can be opened under the scheme.

(3) Maturity:-
(i) The deposit shall mature on the maturity period as prescribed by the Ministry of Finance from time to time as applicable on the date of deposit.
(ii) Current maturity period: 115 months (9 years and 7 months).
(iii) Amount Invested doubles on maturity.

(4) Premature closure:-
KVP may be prematurely closed any time before maturity subject to the following conditions:-(i) On the death of a single holder, or any or all the holders in a joint account. (ii) On forfeiture by a pledgee being a Gazetted officer. (iii) When ordered by court. (iv) After 2 years and 6 months from the date of deposit.

(5) Pledging of account:-
(i) KVP may be pledged or transferred as security, by submitting prescribed application form at concerned Post Office supported with acceptance letter from the pledgee.
(ii) Pledging/transfer can be made to specified authorities.

(6) Transfer of account from one person to another person:-
KVP may be transferred from one person to another person only on the following conditions:-(i) On the death of account holder to nominee/legal heirs. (ii) On the death of account holder to joint holder(s). (iii) On order by the court. (iv) On pledging to specified authorities.
